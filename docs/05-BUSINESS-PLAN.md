# 📈 AthleteView Pro - Business Plan & GTM Strategy

---

## VISION
To become the default broadcast intelligence layer for every live sports event on the planet, starting with India.

## MISSION
Make every athlete a self-contained broadcast unit - delivering richer fan experiences while making coaches smarter and players safer.

## GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

### Year 1: Prove the Tech (2026)
**Target:** 1 league pilot in India
**Best bet:** Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) or ISL - smaller budgets, more innovative
**Why PKL:** Sport uniquely requires athlete proximity - no fixed camera captures the contact/speed adequately
**Deliverable:** 1 team x 11 athletes x 1 full season
**Expected Revenue:** ₹12L hardware + ₹36L broadcast plan = **₹48L ARR**

### Year 2: India Expansion (2027)
**Target:** 3 leagues, 500+ athletes
**Leagues:** PKL + ISL + 1 cricket T20 regional league
**Revenue:** ₹8.5 Cr ARR (hardware + SaaS + streaming)

### Year 3: India Leadership + Global Pilot (2028)
**Target:** All major India leagues + 1 international pilot
**Revenue:** ₹32 Cr ARR
**International:** Middle East (IPL viewership diaspora), UK (cricket/football)
